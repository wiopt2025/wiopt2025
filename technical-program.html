Technical Program This page shows the WiOpt 2024 program, including program at a glance and detailed program. All the times in the program refer to Korean Standard Time (KST, UTC/GMT+9).

Important information for authors: Authors should prepare a 20 min presentation and will have 2-3 additional minutes for answering questions.
