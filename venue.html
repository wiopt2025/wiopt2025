Conference Venue

Important notice about the direction:
If you take the taxi to get to the conference venue, but the taxi driver doesn’t recognize Hana Square, then show the driver the following image, which points to the entrance of Korea University Science Campus. You can follow the sign from the entrance to get to the conference place Hana Square.


The sign from the entrance to get to the conference place Hana Square:


The main conference and workshops will be hosted at Korea University Hana Square (https://maps.app.goo.gl/W4hDuFgF3NgJA2Fi6; 145 Anam-ro, Seongbuk District, Seoul, South Korea), located on the Science Campus of Korea University.
